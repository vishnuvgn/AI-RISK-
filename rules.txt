Part 1 & Part 2 --> (different modes/screens)
Part 1 : Setup 
- take turns placing 1 troop on each territory on the board until all regions have been occupied
- then take turns reinforcing the troops on different regions

Part 2: Gameplay
    Step 1) Recieve new troops (and decide where to place them)
        - can't place in a region that current player doesn't occupy
    Step 2) Attack as long as you want (optional)
    Step 3) Manuever n number of troops from ___ to ____ (optional)
    repeat until game over
